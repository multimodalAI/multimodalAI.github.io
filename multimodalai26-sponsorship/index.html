Call for Sponsorship - MultimodalAI'26

We are pleased to invite you to become a sponsor of the Fourth Workshop on Multimodal AI (MultimodalAI’26), taking place on 11–12 June 2026 at the UCL East Campus, London. Based on strong attendance in previous years, we expect over 120 in-person participants, with capacity for up to 150 attendees from academia, industry, and applied domains.

Multimodal AI integrates diverse types of data, including text, images, audio, time series, graphs, and more. It is rapidly transforming how we engage with information and technology. MultimodalAI’26 will bring together researchers and practitioners from AI, data science, and related scientific and applied fields to discuss challenges, share innovative solutions, explore future collaborations, and strengthen the UK’s growing multimodal AI community.

The workshop is hosted by the UK Open Multimodal AI Network (UKOMAIN), a £1.8 million EPSRC Network Plus initiative. The programme will feature:

  • Keynote presentations by leading experts
  • Community pitches, talks, and poster sessions
  • Engaging panel discussions
  • Updates from open multimodal AI benchmark projects
  • Funding and policy briefings
  • A full-day pre-workshop hackathon on 10 June
  • Travel bursaries and volunteering opportunities to support inclusive participation

Prizes will be awarded for outstanding talks, pitches, posters, and hackathon contributions.

We believe that your mission aligns well with the goals of MultimodalAI’26, and that sponsorship would provide valuable visibility, opportunities for thought leadership, and opportunities to engage with emerging talent and collaborators in the multimodal AI space.

We offer four sponsorship tiers: Platinum (£5,000), Gold (£3,500), Silver (£2,000), and Bronze (£1,000). The benefits for each tier are outlined below. We are also happy to discuss bespoke options, such as sponsored sessions, branded materials, or tailored hackathon engagement.

Platinum Sponsors (£5,000)

  • Invited 10-minute talk on a topic related to multimodal AI
  • Eight complimentary registrations
  • Priority exhibition stand at the registration and/or reception area
  • Banner display at registration and reception areas
  • Prominent banner or signage display at the hackathon venue
  • Recognition during prize award announcements (logo display)
  • Organisation name, logo, and half-page profile in the workshop programme
  • Prominent signage during one lunch session and one poster session
  • Logo and URL featured on workshop webpages

Gold Sponsors (£3,500)

  • Invited 5-minute short talk on a topic related to multimodal AI
  • Six complimentary registrations
  • Exhibition stand at the registration and/or reception area
  • Banner display at registration and/or reception areas
  • Signage or banner display at the hackathon venue
  • Recognition during prize award announcements (logo display)
  • Prominent signage during one lunch or poster session
  • Organisation name, logo, and a 200-word profile in the workshop programme
  • Logo and URL featured on workshop webpages

Silver Sponsors (£2,000)

  • Invited 1-minute pitch relevant to multimodal AI
  • Four complimentary registrations
  • Exhibition stand at the registration area (subject to availability)
  • Organisation name, logo, and a 100-word profile in the workshop programme
  • Logo and URL featured on workshop webpages

Bronze Sponsors (£1,000)

  • Two complimentary registrations
  • Organisation name and logo in the workshop programme
  • Logo and URL featured on workshop webpages

To express interest or discuss sponsorship options further, please contact us at ukomain-mmai26@googlegroups.com.

Thank you for considering this opportunity. We would be delighted to welcome you as a sponsor of MultimodalAI’26.
